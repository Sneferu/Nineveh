Linux Device Guide (compatible with udev)

Finding Devices ---------------------------------------------------------------

To list PCI devs:
    lspci

To list usb devs:
    lsusb

To list block devs:
    lsblk

Devices -----------------------------------------------------------------------

To see current RAM usage:
    free

Filesystems -------------------------------------------------------------------

To list disks:
    fdisk -l

To see partition UUID:
    blkid
